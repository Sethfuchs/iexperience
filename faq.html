Why is it called iExperience?

We want you to have an experience, not just spend the whole time coding etc etc

Do I need to know how to program?

No. All you need is passion and the ability to unlearn the word "quit."

If I already know how to program a little, is iExperience for me?

Absolutely. iExerience is more than just learning how to program – it's learning how to be a coder. It's learning how to build your own prototypes. It's learning where to go to find answers. It's learning about what the atmosphere of working in a startup is really like. Its about experience technology in a new culture.
