Next Steps

Below you will find the next steps to have the experience of a lifetime

Step 1

iExperience is not a typical classroom. Think of it more as an intensive workshop. You will be learning by doing and building, not just by sitting back and listening.

What you can expect
Pair Programming
Lots of collaboration
Hand coded XHTML and CSS markup
The ability to build you own App

Step 2

We believe in work hard, play hard. Surfboards and mountain bikes are on the walls of the iExperience center. Grab one and take a break. Inspiration is found in nature and the beauty that surrounds us.

We encourage
An active lifestyle
Cultural exploration
Interaction with locals
Healthy snacks

Step 3

We are placing you to intern at local tech companies that are making a big impact in the society we live in. You will work on their development team and contribute to their projects with the skills you have learned.

Cool companies we work with
Greenpop
Trashback
Citymob
Platform 45
