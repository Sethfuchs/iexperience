What is the program?

The program is a 8 week, full time summer program, designed to turn you into a web developer. The goal is to give you the equivalent skillset and experience of an entry-level Ruby developer. That means you will be equipped with all the tools needed to build web applications. You don't need any prior web experience to be able to attend, just the right attitude and desire to have a summer experience of a lifetime.

A typical day at iExperience labs

7 AM

Breakfast at the Lodge. Possible walk on Table Mountain or a dip in the sea.

9 AM

Arrive at school and review yesterday’s work. Instructor explains today’s concept: ActiveRecord.

NOON

Lunch with the class at one of Cape Town's best burger joints - Clarke's

1 - 4 PM

Break up into groups and work on a new app idea that uses data to suggest a song based on the mood of everyone in the class.

4 - 6 PM

Day over. You decide to stay at the office to plug icons into your app. Then happy hour at Caprice in Camps Bay with classmates. Back to work? Yes.

9:30 PM

Dinner or a movie at the historic Labia cinema around the corner, in Orange Street?
