What is the program?

The program is an 8 week, full-time summer program, designed to turn you into a web developer. A 4 week intensive coding bootcamp, followed by a 4 week hands-on internship at a local tech company. The goal is to give you the equivalent skillset and experience of an entry-level Ruby developer. That means after the program, you will be equipped with all the tools needed to build web applications. You don't need any prior web experience to be able to attend, just a do-it-yourself attitude and desire to have a summer experience of a lifetime.
