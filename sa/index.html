Our Vission

iExperience is a tech-education academy that believes in teaching valuable skills to upcoming entrepreneurs in South Africa. Coding, web design and entrepreneurial thinking are an integral part of economic development and growth in our society. Our dream is to empower thinkers and entrepreneurs by offering full-time immersive courses on the most relevant skills of the 21st century.

About the Program

iExperience offers an immersive 4-week programming boot camp designed to take you from a novice, with zero technical experience, to a self-sufficient web developer able to build your own Ruby on Rails web applications. The full-time course, conducted in partnership with Platform45, is led by experienced instructors and includes lectures, self-work, pair-programming, weekly code review sessions, projects and more. Upon completion of the course, you will have learned a complete web stack and have the ability to build your own project, as well as gaining the knowledge of how to further your education alone.

5 more reasons to learn code

Jobs are abundant...

Every company is a software company. Today, more than ever, the amount of development jobs are increasing to a point where it is impossible to fill the demand.

Know the language. Know the people...

Understanding the language & concerns of web developers enables you to have a more effective interaction with them. Knowing what your developers do will help you scale projects & prioritise tasks.

Automation will power your business...

Programming will enable you to write scripts to take care of mundane tasks while you focus on the important aspects of your business.

Build a web presence...

Whether you are promoting your personal brand or creating your company’s web presence, you will be able to build the sites you want, when you want them.

Become a problem solver...

Learning to program trains your mind to think about problems and solutions in a different way. Don’t think outside the box, throw it away.

What you will learn

The best way to learn is by doing. We will have you coding, and coding… and coding!

HTML and CSS

Core fundamental skills to the world of web development which you will master by the end of this program.

Javascript and JQuery

Bring the web to life with jQuery-powered interactions and an understanding of the core of JavaScript.

Ruby on Rails

Learn one of the best ways to build modern, database-backed web applications

Databases

Relational or Document Store? MySQL or Postgres? Mongo or Redis? Cloud or dev/null? Learning the fundamentals to data storage is a must. And yes, you will know SQL.

Agile Development

Agile is more than just a development practice, it's a philosophy for life. Develop and iterate on your projects by keeping a close eye on costs and value.

Product Management

Product management lies at the intersection between business, design, and technology; giving you the foresight to gauge whether a product is viable from a business perspective.

Terminal / Unix

Terminal (or command line) is your home base. From here, you take full control over the way your files are created and used, and the way your app is put together.

Git - Source Control

Manage your code versions, and collaborate on projects with other programmers.
