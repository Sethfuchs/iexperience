About the Program

You'll go from zero tech experience to self-sufficient web developer, able to design and build your own web apps - in just four intensive weeks. The full-time course, conducted in partnership with Platform45, is led by experienced instructors and includes lectures, self-work, pair-programming, weekly code review sessions, projects and more. Upon completion of the course, you will have learned a complete web stack and have the ability to build your own app, as well as gaining the knowledge of how to further your education alone.

Databases

Relational or Document Store? MySQL or Postgres? Mongo or Redis? Cloud or dev/null? Learning the fundamentals to data storage is a must. And yes, you will know SQL.

Agile Development

Agile is more than just a development practice, it's a philosophy for life. Develop and iterate on your projects by keeping a close eye on costs and value.

Product Management

Product management lies at the intersection between business, design, and technology; giving you the foresight to gauge whether a product is viable from a business perspective.

Terminal / Unix

Terminal (or command line) is your home base. From here, you take full control over the way your files are created and used, and the way your app is put together.

Git - Source Control
